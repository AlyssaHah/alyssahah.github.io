GIMM Studio Space: Concept art and Outfits

For a collaborative final project, some of my classmates and I worked on creating a virtual learning environment to be used for teaching purposes for future GIMM courses. I was put in charge of creating concept art for the character avatars that students will use when they enter the virtual space, as well as art for various outfits and cosmetics that they could equipt for their avatar. I also had the opportunity to model and texture a few of my designs!

'Happy Cat!' Models

This is a playblast animation of a 3D cat tail that I modeled and textured. I used Maya to model, rig, and animate the tail, and I used Photoshop for the texturing.
